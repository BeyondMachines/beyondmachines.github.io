Our latest challenge is ByteBreach 2024

ByteBreach is a scavenger hunt. Find the link to the next clue, and collect secrets to confirm you have found each clue.

The final challenge will provide an email to send the solution. Make sure you include a good explanation of your solution.

We will be selecting two awards:

  • First Prize:
    • Participation in the development of the challenge as a penetration tester
    • Mentorship to develop penetration testing skills, including building skills and writing pentest reports
    • The final pentest will serve as a portfolio of a completed pentest
  • Second Prize:
    • Attendance at the workshop: "The Day After Production - How It Feels When Your Platform Gets Hacked in Real Time"
    • Workshop will be held at Base42 on December 14, 2024
